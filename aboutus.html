About Us

Bountiful Foods was established in 1985 here in Carlsbad California. The idea to begin a local produce farm came from the idea that everyone should have access to real fresh food. Bountiful Foods works closely with local farmers to deliver to distributors and anyone who wants healthy options. Let our farms be considered your backyard garden. We have a store front location here, and also deliver from LA to San Diego.
